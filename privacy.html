Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

No personal data is collected.

Luminaera ThreadJumper processes all AI conversation data entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded, transmitted, shared, or stored on any external server.

Scope

This privacy policy applies to all current versions of the ThreadJumper browser extension.

Data Collection and Usage

ThreadJumper does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track browsing activity
  • Transmit data to remote servers
  • Use third-party analytics, tracking, or telemetry services

Local Usage Data

ThreadJumper may store limited usage information locally on your device, such as feature usage counts or remaining usage time, solely for license activation and feature availability purposes.

This information:

  • Is stored entirely on your device
  • Is not transmitted or shared externally
  • Is not linked to personal identity
  • Is used only to enforce licensing and access limits

Local usage data is designed to be transparent and accessible within the extension interface. Users can review and understand what the tool remembers about their usage.

Permissions

ThreadJumper requests only the minimum browser permissions required to provide its features. These permissions are used strictly for on-device functionality and never for data collection, tracking, or transmission.

External Services

The extension may include an optional link to a third-party payment service (such as Gumroad) for purchasing an activation key. Clicking this link will redirect you to the service provider’s website, which is governed by its own privacy policy.

Future Features

Future versions of ThreadJumper may introduce optional local usage statistics. If implemented, these features will:

  • Run entirely on your device
  • Generate reports visible only to you
  • Allow you to manually share reports if you choose

Any such data sharing would be strictly optional, initiated by the user, and performed outside the extension itself.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published on this page. Privacy commitments evolve cautiously and transparently.
